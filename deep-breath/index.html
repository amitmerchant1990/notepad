Deep Breath

A simple breathing exercise (also called "square breathing") to help you de-stress yourself.

You can also use this to unwind yourself after a long writing session.

Get relaxed with four easy steps:

  • Breathe in deeply for around four seconds
  • Hold your breath for around four seconds
  • Breathe out for around six seconds
  • Hold your breath for around four seconds

Repeat this for a few minutes or until you start feeling better.

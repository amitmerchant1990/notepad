Case Converter

This simple case converter tool allows you to convert any text between various letter cases, including...

  • Lower case
  • Upper case
  • Sentence case
  • Capitalized case
  • Alternating case
  • Title case
  • Inverse case

Simply enter your text, select the desired case, and click "Convert".

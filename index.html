    This is an offline-capable Notepad which is a Progressive Web App as well.

    The app serves the following features:

    • Lets you write notes in plain text which are autosaved locally.
    • Installable on supported browsers for offline usage.
    • Dark mode.
    • Privacy-focused ‚Äî Never collects your precious data.
    • Light-weight ‚Äî Loads almost instantly.
    • Full-screen mode for a distraction-free writing experience.
    • Optimal line length for better reading experience.
    • Floating window (in supported browsers) to effectively take notes across other apps.
    • Download notes as PDF or plain text.
    • It's proudly open-source!

    CAUTION: Take a backup of your notes more often since it's all stored on your browser.

    Rich Text Notes

    This is a simple tool to write in rich text with a bunch of formatting options.

    It serves the following features:

    • Lets you write notes with formatting if you choose it like that and saves it locally in real time as you type.
    • Download your notes as HTML and PDF.
    • Blazing fast and lightweight.
    • Support for adding images and videos.

    CAUTION: Take a backup of your notes more often since it's all stored on your browser.
